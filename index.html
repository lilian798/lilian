Admissions

Thank you for considering Faulu School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to ensure a smooth and positive experience for both students and parents.

Admission Process

To apply for admission, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents, including academic transcripts and recommendation letters.
  3. Attend an admissions interview, if applicable.

Admission Requirements

We welcome students who demonstrate:

  • Academic excellence
  • Curiosity and eagerness to learn
  • Positive character and behavior

Application Form

Complete the online application form to initiate the admission process. We look forward to welcoming your child to Faulu School.
